Portfolio Overview

57 holdings across 7 sleeves — KKP Wealth V2 Expanded managed portfolio (Version 2 - Port 1+)

The Idea Behind This Portfolio

KKP Wealth V2 Expanded is a balanced-growth revision of the Expanded portfolio. It retains the core defensive structure (gold + cash ≈ 41%) while modestly increasing equity exposure for better long-term growth. Key changes: mandate halved (37.6% → 18.8%), standalone PIMCO GIS sleeve, PTT local equity added (3.66%), Vista PE for vintage diversification, and expanded satellite with Europe + India themes. Expected return: ~7% p.a. with lower volatility than V2 Port 1.

Fixed Income (6.18%)
Standalone PIMCO GIS Global Bond — active flexible bond management. Smaller than V2 Port 1 (11.82%) as capital shifts to equity, but provides flexible global bond exposure.
Equity (4.80%)
VAYU1 Thai equity (1.14%) plus PTT direct stock (3.66%) — new concentrated Thai energy holding for dividend yield and local exposure.
Mandate (18.81%)
KKP Balanced Standard (16.2% offshore) and KKP Aggressive THB (2.61% onshore) — halved from Expanded's 37.6%, reducing professional management coverage.
Alternative (13.78%)
Lazard Infrastructure (4.18%), Hedge Funds (3.13%), KKR K-PRIME (3.13%), and Vista PE VIII (3.34%) — expanded alternatives with new hedge fund and Vista PE allocations.
Commodities (14.63%)
Physical Gold Bullions — retained from Expanded. Provides crisis alpha, inflation hedge, and portfolio stabilization. Major distinction from V2 Port 1.
Satellite (15.66%)
Thematic equity (5.22%), ACWI Passive (5.22%), Capital Group Active (4.18%), and DO Notes (1.04%) — expanded with India and Europe themes.
Cash (26.12%)
Fixed Savings — retained from Expanded. Provides stability, liquidity for opportunistic rebalancing, and downside protection. THB-denominated fixed deposit.

V2 Expanded is an evolutionary update, not a revolution. Gold + cash buffer preserved at ~41%, equity modestly increased for growth. Less dramatic risk shift than V2 Port 1.
